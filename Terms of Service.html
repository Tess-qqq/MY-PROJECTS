GrindCore Accountability Terms

Last Updated: July 2023

⚠️ This is not a social server. All interactions must relate directly to personal accountability and improvement tracking.

1. Acceptable Use

You agree to use this system solely for:

  • Posting structured self-improvement plans
  • Tracking measurable progress
  • Participating in the TRP accountability system

2. TRP System Rules

  1. Honesty: False progress reports will reset your DP to 0
  2. Consistency: 7+ days inactivity reduces TRP by 5% weekly
  3. Verification: Progress requires peer reactions (✅/❌)

3. Prohibited Actions

  • Gaming the TRP system
  • Posting non-accountability content
  • Abusing reaction features
  • Creating duplicate accounts

4. Consequences

Violations may result in:

  • TRP reset
  • Temporary mute
  • Permanent ban

5. Changes

Rules evolve with the community. Major changes will be announced 7 days in advance.

6. Contact
